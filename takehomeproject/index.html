All About the Harp

Playing Harp in the Past

How I began Harp

I started playing the harp when I was 15. I had a friend that played the harp, and I was always jealous of her. I begged my parents to let me take lessons, and finally they agreed. As soon as I started learning the harp, I discovered that I loved it! I continued lessons all through highschool.

Studying Harp in School

I was briefly a music major, but I decided I wanted to keep harp as a hobby.

Playing Harp Now

KSU Symphony Orchestra

Even though I am no longer a music major, I still love the harp. I have started playing in the KSU Symphony Orchestra! This weekend I have a concert. It is challenging being the only harpist in the orchestra, but I enjoy it.

Competitions

Throughout my time learning the harp, I have done a few competitions. When I was 18 I won an international music competition. Currently I am looking at entering a new competition. It will be more difficult, because I will be in a higher age division. But I find that regardless of how the competition goes, I find the experience motivating.

Fact about the Harp

A concert grand harp has 47 strings!
